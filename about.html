About Sanskrit

A journey through authentic Indian flavours

Our Story

A Culinary Journey from India to Newcastle

Sanskrit Restaurant was born from a deep passion for sharing the authentic flavours of India with the vibrant community of Newcastle upon Tyne. Our name, Sanskrit, represents the ancient language that forms the foundation of Indian culture and philosophy - much like how traditional recipes and techniques form the foundation of our cuisine.

Since our establishment, we have been committed to bringing you an unparalleled culinary experience that transports you to the bustling streets and aromatic kitchens of India. Every dish tells a story, every spice has a purpose, and every meal is a celebration of India's rich culinary heritage.

Our chefs, trained in the traditional techniques passed down through generations, combine authentic recipes with the finest ingredients to create dishes that are both true to their origins and exciting to the modern palate.

Our Promise

Authentic Flavours, Modern Experience

Imagine the aroma of sizzling pakoras mingling with the gentle fragrance of cardamom chai. Close your eyes and hear the rhythmic clatter of spatulas on tawa, a melody promising culinary delights. This isn't your average meal; it's a journey to the heart of India, served at Sanskrit.

Each bite sings of ancient traditions, passed down through generations, whispering secrets of fresh spices and slow-cooked perfection. Let your taste buds become your passport, savoring the vibrant tapestry of India one flavorful mouthful at a time.

Our Commitment

Beyond Just Food

At Sanskrit, we believe dining is about more than just food - it's about creating memories, celebrating culture, and bringing people together. Our commitment extends beyond serving delicious meals to creating an experience that honors the rich traditions of Indian hospitality.

We are dedicated to:

  • Maintaining the highest standards of food safety and hygiene

  • Preserving and sharing authentic Indian culinary traditions

Whether you're joining us for a casual lunch, romantic dinner, or special celebration, we promise to make your visit to Sanskrit a memorable journey through the flavors of India.
